# Ahoy Captain!

![Accordia](/img/accordia.png)

#### Seabeard is the fun-packed adventure game where you can save the world, or simply hang out and enjoy the view.

### The adventure begins on the island of Accordia...

![Bickering](/img/seabeard-bicker-and-brawl.png)

#### The oceans of Okabu were once home to many rival tribes who did nothing but bicker and brawl.

![](/img/seabeard_bird.png)

#### The great explorer Seabeard (your grandfather) dreamed of one day uniting the tribes…

![](/img/seabeard_harmony.png)

#### …by building a great city that would usher in a new era of peace and prosperity.

## Sadly fate had other plans


![](/img/monster.png)

#### The great new city of Accordia was threatened with ruin by an ancient monster that had risen from the depths.

![Battle](/img/intro_crew.png)

#### Without hesitation, Seabeard set sail with his faithful crew to battle the leviathan…

### …but the beast was unstoppable.

![](/img/ruins.png)

#### As Accordia crumbled, so too did the bonds between the tribes. Seabeard was gone and, it seemed, all hope and goodwill with him.

#### As Seabeard’s only descendant, you must restore **Accordia to its former glory** and **reunite the people** of this great land!
